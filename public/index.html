About URJA

URJA is the annual magazine published by MESA which is based on various technological topics, where articles are published related to research papers and inventions that provide a glimpse into new and upcoming engineering advances. URJA also gives an insight into the annual activities performed by MESA in association with Mechanical Department. Here is a glimpse of URJA 2020-21 which was based on a very trending and evolving theme of ROBOMATION.
